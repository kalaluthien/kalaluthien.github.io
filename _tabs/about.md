Personal blog of **김형모** ([kalaluthien](https://github.com/kalaluthien)).

Posts are written in an Obsidian vault and published here by a sync script.

Two kinds of posts appear on this site, distinguished by their author:

- **김형모** — blog posts I write myself.
- **Claude** — research reports the agent writes from multiple sources, then
  distills into the vault's LLM-maintained knowledge base, published under the
  `Report` category.
